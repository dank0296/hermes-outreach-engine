## 4. What capital can reasonably be raised

### A) Angel check from Banda alone (working demo, early)
| Style | Range | Notes |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Friends & family / angel | **$50k–$150k** | Common single-check if belief is high |
| Serious angel | **$150k–$300k** | Fits “funds to back the project” narrative |
| Stretch single check | **$300k–$500k** | Only if he wants meaningful ownership + is liquid |

Instrument: **Post-money SAFE** (YC-style), discussion cap often **$4M–$8M** at this stage (demo, thin revenue).  
AI SaaS pre-seed *rounds* often **$750k–$1.5M** — that’s a **round**, not one angel unless he leads a syndicate.

### B) If Banda **leads / syndicates** his operator network as angels
| Round | Range | Cap discussion |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| Micro pre-seed | **$400k–$750k** | $6–10M |
| Standard pre-seed (AI SaaS comps) | **$750k–$1.5M** | $10–15M median-ish market |

### C) Non-dilutive / hybrid with channel
- Smaller equity check (**$100–200k**) **+** formal channel agreement  
- Aligns capital with his **distribution** (friends who run servers)
