# Banda meeting — dual track (customer + investor)

**Context:** Banda may bring **capital**, not only a Discord to run.  
He already has / will have the **investor brief**. Demo still leads; money talk follows interest.

---

## How to run the room

| Phase | Time | Goal |
|-------|------|------|
| 1. Working model | 10–20 min | Prove product (Part A optional, Part B visual) |
| 2. Silence | 10s | Let him react |
| 3. Dual ask | 2 min | Customer **or** capital **or** both |
| 4. Next step only | 3 min | Book follow-up — no term sheet live |

**Do not** pitch valuation on first demo.  
**Do not** hand repo keys / full deploy as “investment diligence” without process.

---

## Lines (muscle memory)

### Open (same as always)
> “Working model: agent works cold 24/7; you only get pinged when a lead is worth it.”

### After handoff moment
> “That’s the product loop. Same loop is what we sell as operator seats — and what content can scale.”

### Dual-track fork (critical)
> “Two ways this can go with you:  
> **A)** You run this for your community as a pilot seat.  
> **B)** You’re looking at this as something bigger to back.  
> **C)** Both.  
> Which is more interesting after what you just saw?”

### If he goes investor
> “Great. Next call is commercial — scope, use of funds, what design-partner looks like.  
> This meeting stays product proof. I won’t dump IP on a first pass.”

### If he goes customer only
> “Perfect — pilot dry-run with your ICP, operator seat when you’re ready for live channels.”

### If he wants to resell to his whole network
> “Distribution partnership we can structure. Platform stays with us — that’s how it stays supported and valuable.”
